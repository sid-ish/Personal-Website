The Work

Art & Photography

Digital illustration, traditional ink, and photography. Three different tools for the same obsession — noticing things.

+ Add digital art: Drop files into images/digital/ then add one line to the catalogue.digital array in js/main.js
+ Add traditional art: Drop files into images/traditional/ then add one line to the catalogue.traditional array in js/main.js
+ Add photos: Drop files into images/photography/ then add one line to the catalogue.photography array in js/main.js
The Blog

Thoughts & Things

Where I write about what I'm building, what I'm noticing, and what's stuck in my head. No schedule. Just when something's worth saying.

+ Write a new post: Create a .txt file in the thoughts/ folder using this format:

title: My Post Title
date: April 2026
tag: Art
emoji: 🎨
excerpt: Short summary for the card.
---
Your full post text goes here. Blank lines = new paragraphs. ## for headings. > for quotes.

Then add the filename to the thoughtFiles array in js/main.js — that's it.
The Person

Who I Actually Am

I'm a second-year Mechanical Engineering student at SSN College of Engineering in Chennai. The honest version: I'm someone who gets obsessed with making things real. If I can imagine it, I want it to exist.

During the week I'm building autonomous robots, writing embedded code, and figuring out how drones stay in the air. On the weekends I'm drawing characters, taking photos of rain on leaves, or building something nobody asked for.

The name i_wreck_things started as a joke. Now it's accurate. Breaking stuff is how you understand it.
